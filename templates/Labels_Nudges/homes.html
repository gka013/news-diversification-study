News Research: Climate Change

Welcome

Welcome to our study! This study aims to provide insight in the extent to which recommender systems can be effective in mitigating polarized opinions. Procedure

You will be first asked to answer a few questions about your personal characteristics and attitude toward climate change. Next, you will be presented with a list of ten news items. Afterwards, you will be asked a few questions after reading the article.

Duration

The study takes around 10 minutes to complete. By clicking the 'Start' button, you agree to have read and understood this consent form.
